Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Nuzvid (RGUKT-Nuzvid) was founded as a State University by an act of the Legislature by AP Government as a full-fledged university in in 2008. The primary objective of establishing RGUKT was to provide high quality educational opportunities for the aimed rural youth of Andhra Pradesh. The mission of RGUKT-N is to use advances in learning sciences and explore the use of modern cognitive science tools in education and learning
